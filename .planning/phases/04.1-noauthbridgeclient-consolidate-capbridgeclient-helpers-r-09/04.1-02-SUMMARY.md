verify 74 green without test-file edits, (b) trim newly dead imports in
`x9.client.ts`, (c) update the outdated forward-reference comment block
at L18-31. The plan anticipated these as a separate "cleanup" commit after
verifying 02-01 landed green.

In practice (a)/(b)/(c) were a single atomic edit in task 02-01 because:

  describes the new body.

Verification (a) still ran: all 74 tests passed without test-file edits.

includes the full cleanup scope that 02-02 would have added. An empty
